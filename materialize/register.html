Becoming a member of the Libertarian Party of Duval County, FL is very simple. You just need to be an active registered LPF voter and live in Duval County, FL.

You can check your voter registration by visiting the Division of Elections website. Fill out the form and check where it says "Party/Partido". It must say "Libertarian Party of Florida." We need your birthdate to check this information for verification.
